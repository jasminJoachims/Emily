My dear Beloved

,

I wouldst liketh to inviteth thee to a date at twelve o'clock thy timeth tom'rrow, so yond we can gaze "The Princess Switch III" togeth'r.

I expecteth thee to calleth at five to twelve, so we may beginneth most carefully upon the hour.

I suggesteth thee cometh equipp'd with a cuppeth of tea and an appropriate sweet of thy choosing.

I shalt look f'rward to seeing thee tom'rrow.

Most faithfully,

Jas
